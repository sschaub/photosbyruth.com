Are you looking for an affordable, attractive idea for a gift? Or just a simple card without a cheesy message?

These note cards, printed on quality 80 lb. Xpressions card stock, just might be what you're looking for! There are several collections from which to choose, or you can even build your own package with the option found at the bottom of the page. And if you really like a certain picture and would like a custom message with that picture, feel free to contact me at rnschaub@gmail.com.

The blank note cards, which come with a white envelope, are 4.25 x 5.5 inches. Each package of 8 is only $6.00 (plus tax and shipping).
